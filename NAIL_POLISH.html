DEFINITION & USE
Its a paint that can be put on your nails.

CREATE
The chemicals are mixed together, heated, and poured into the bottle.

TRASH AND RECYLE
Nail polish is full of toxic chemicals so it should not be thrown into the trash can or down the drain. It is considered Household Hazardous Waste. You should call your local Hazardous Waste Facility to schedule a drop off. It is made with a lot of chemicals. Nail polish contains three main hazardous chemicals such Dibutyl phthalate, formaldehyde and toluene and other toxic chemicals. Effects of Nail Polish in our Water Systems: These chemical are can cause, cancer, chronic disease, asthma, and birth defects.
