DEFINITION & USE
LEDs (Light Emitting Diodes) are solid light bulbs that are very energy-efficient.

They are often found in instrument panels, electronics, pen lights, strings of indoor and outdoor Christmas lights, and home lighting.

RECYCLE
LED lights should not be recycled in regular bin but you can recycle them at area drop-off places. Check with your local recycling facility.

TRASH
Throwing away LED lights are dangerous because they have hazardous materials like nickel, some lead, and even trace amounts of arsenic. There are significant health hazards to throwing them in a landfill.
