DEFINITION & USE
A plastic curtain that keeps water from splashing out of the shower.

CREATE
First a plastic from a roll is cut into 2 yard sections, then a special machine punches 12 holes in the top of the plastic in order to make it possible to hang it in your bathroom.

RECYCLE
PVC is not accepted by recycling facilities. However, you must reuse shower curtains at home in many fun ways! You can use a plastic shower curtain and turn them into water slides.You can use them as a BBQ cover. You can use the fabric shower curtains as curtains for your bed room.

TRASH
Many shower curtains and shower curtain are made with harmful material called PVC which release a toxic gas. PVC stands for Polyvinyl Chloride. PVC is the most toxic plastic known. PVC can cause cancer, birth defects, diabetes, learning and developmental delays, endometriosis, and immune system abnormalities.
