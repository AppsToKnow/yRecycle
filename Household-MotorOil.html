DEFINITION & USE
You use motor oil for the main function is to reduce wear on moving parts; it also cleans, inhibits corrosion, improves sealing, and cools the engine by carrying heat away from moving parts.

It is made of hydrocarbons, polyalphaolefins , and polyalphaolefins with is made of carbon and hydrogen.

CREATE
You take 77.50% base oil and 20% additives to make motor oi

RECYCLE
Yes bring it to an autoparts store for recycling.

TRASH
Do not throw into the trash can, pour down the drain, or into the sewer. Take to your local autoparts store.
