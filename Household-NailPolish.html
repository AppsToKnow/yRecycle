DEFINITION & USE
Its a paint that can be put on your nails. It is made with a lot of chemicals. Nitrocellulose is a common one.

CREATE
The chemicals are mixed together, heated, and poured into the bottle.

RECYCLE
It is considered Household Hazardous Waste. You should call your local Hazardous Waste Facility to schedule a drop off.

TRASH
Tail polish is full of toxic chemicals so it should not be thrown into the trash can or down the drain.
