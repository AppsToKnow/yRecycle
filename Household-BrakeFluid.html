DEFINITION & USE
Brake fluid is made of DOT fluid or mineral fluid. It is used for Hydraulic brakes and clutch applications. It is used in Automobiles, Motorcycles, light trucks and bicycles.

CREATE
The people who make it boil the DOT fluid to the boiling temperature and then it will be put in a bottle.

RECYCLE
Contact your local autoparts store or city waste management disposal facility. Do NOT put your brake fluid in your curbside recycle bin.

TRASH
Brake fluid is alcohol-based. It's toxic when ingested. It is also very flammable. Never throw it out or dump it down your drain.

To dispose of new or unused brake fluid, pour it into a container of cat litter. The brake fluid will evaporate within a few days.
