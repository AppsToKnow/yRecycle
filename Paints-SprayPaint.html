DEFINITION & USE
Spray paint is paint in a spray can and is usually used to finish off projects and is also used to make public art like grafitti.

CREATE
Spray paint is made to shoot paint in a mist like form. The container is made with aluminum and plastic (for the cap) and is filled with different colors of paint.

RECYCLE
Yes, you must empty out the can completely or else the paint can leak out and sink into the ground and could pollute soil and prevent plants from growing.

Empty spray paint cans can be placed in curbside recycling programs.

If they still have paint in them DO NOT place them in recycling bins. Instead contact your local recycling program.

TRASH
Theses paint cans are to be completely emptied out and recycled.
