DEFINITION & USE
When your trying to get coffee fast you go to fast food places, they don't give you a ceramic coffee mug, usually they give you a paper cup made of foamy polystyrene. Polystyrene is what most people call styrofoam. Styrofoam has a light weight and is about 95% air .

RECYCLE
No, you cannot recycle them because fast food coffee cups have plastic or wax lining which helps you but not the environment. Plastic or wax lining helps with temperature control, but it also makes them non-recyclable. Some communities accept paper cups ,but its usually to difficult separate during the process. Starbucks is trying to lead the way with their recyclable cups by 2015.
