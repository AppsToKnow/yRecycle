DEFINITION & USE
Wood stains are like paint that you can use to make something darker or make it look better.You usually put it on wood for when it is there for a long time and the color is dying you use that to give it life again.

CREATE
Wood stains are made by oils,dyes and pigments mixed together to make something like a paint to make a color richer. They are dissolved in a solvent which then creates the stain

RECYCLE
This item can not be recycled.

TRASH
Read the label and determine if it is considered a hazardous material. NEVER dump it down a drain or into a sewer. Instead call your local hazardous waste center and schedule a drop off.

Or you can put the extra stain in a can then add cat litter and let it harden. After a while you can just throw in the trash can. Also the best of all it is safe for the environment.
